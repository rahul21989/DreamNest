DreamNest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version

DreamNest does not collect, store, or sell any personal information. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. The only data that ever leaves your device is the story prompt you type — sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a bedtime story — and it contains no personal identifiers.

1. Who We Are

DreamNest is a bedtime companion app for children and their parents. This Privacy Policy explains what data is handled when you use the app and how it is treated.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No Apple ID or device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No payment or financial information
  • No data about your child

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server:

  • Routines & settings — saved in the app's local UserDefaults
  • Saved stories — stored in the app's local Documents folder
  • Recorded lullabies — audio files saved in the app's local Documents folder
  • Parent PIN — stored in local UserDefaults on your device only

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

4. Google Gemini API

When you create a bedtime story, the title, optional theme, and the creative prompt you type are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate and narrate the story. This request contains no personal information about you or your child.

Google's handling of API requests is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the Gemini API Terms of Service.

If you do not use the Create Story feature, no data is ever sent outside your device.

5. Microphone Access

DreamNest requests microphone permission only to allow parents to record a personal lullaby. Recordings are processed and saved entirely on-device. No audio is ever transmitted to any server or third party.

6. Children's Privacy

DreamNest is designed for use by parents on behalf of young children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app contains no social features, no user-generated content sharing, and no in-app purchases that require personal data.

7. Third-Party Services

DreamNest does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media SDKs. The only external service used is the Google Gemini API, described in Section 4.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
