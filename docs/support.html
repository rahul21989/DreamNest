Getting Started

How do I play a lullaby?

Open the Home tab, tap any category card (e.g. Lullabies), then tap the play button next to any track. The music player appears at the bottom of the screen.

How do I set a sleep timer?

While a track is playing, tap "Sleep Timer & Routine" in the player bar at the bottom. Choose any duration (1, 2, 5, 10, or 15 minutes). The music will fade out gently when the timer ends. For an instant start, tap the "Quick Sleep" button in the Home screen header.

How do I create a bedtime story?

  1. Tap the Story tab
  2. Enter a story title
  3. Optionally select a world (Space, Animals, Ocean, etc.)
  4. Type a short idea in the prompt field (e.g. "A brave bunny finds a hidden garden")
  5. Tap Create My Story
  6. The story appears in seconds — tap Listen to Story to hear it read aloud

How do I save a story for offline reading?

After generating a story, tap the Save button in the top-right corner. Saved stories are available in the Story tab by tapping the book icon, even without an internet connection.

How do I record my own lullaby?

  1. Go to Home tab → tap the Lullabies category
  2. Tap Record
  3. Allow microphone access when prompted
  4. Sing or speak, then tap Stop
  5. Your recording appears under "My Recordings"

How do I access the Parents area?

Go to the Settings tab and tap Open Parents. You'll be asked to set a PIN on first access. This area lets you manage bedtime routines, adjust sleep timer defaults, and delete recordings.

Troubleshooting

The story isn't generating — what do I do?

Story generation requires an internet connection. Please check your connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact us at goyal021989@gmail.com.

The Listen button isn't working

Story narration requires an internet connection to generate the audio. Make sure you are connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data, then tap Listen to Story again.

Microphone recording doesn't start

DreamNest needs microphone permission to record lullabies. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone on your iPhone and make sure DreamNest is enabled.

I forgot my Parent PIN

Go to Settings → Clear Data. This resets all settings including the PIN. Note: this also clears saved routines and settings, but does not delete saved stories or recordings.

How do I delete a recording?

Go to Home → Lullabies → My Recordings. Swipe left on any recording and tap Delete. You can also access this from the Parents area.

Privacy

Does DreamNest collect any personal data?

No. DreamNest does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data (stories, settings, recordings) stays on your device. The only external call is the story prompt sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a story — this contains no personal identifiers. Read our full Privacy Policy.

Is this app safe for children?

Yes. DreamNest has no social features, no user accounts, no advertising, and no in-app purchases. All content is reviewed for age-appropriateness. The app is designed for supervised use by parents with young children.
